Our Services

Restaurant

We offer the best traditional Moroccan cuisine in a welcoming atmosphere. Our restaurant is also the perfect venue for your special events, combining delicious food, expert service, and a unique cultural experience.

Caterer

Our catering service offers a range of exquisite menu options, tailored to your event’s needs. Whether it’s traditional Moroccan cuisine or international dishes, we ensure every meal is prepared with the finest ingredients, presented beautifully, and served with exceptional attention to detail.

Decoration

We offer beautiful and customizable event decorations to bring your vision to life. From elegant centerpieces to themed setups, our team ensures every detail is perfect, creating a stunning atmosphere that enhances your special occasion.

Orchestra

Our orchestration service ensures your event flows effortlessly from start to finish. We coordinate every aspect, from timing and transitions to vendor management, making sure all elements come together seamlessly for a flawless experience.

“Negafa”

Our costume management service provides tailored solutions for every occasion, offering outfits for you, your guests, fitting arrangements, design and fitting for rentals and custom creations, to handle all the details to make sure everyone looks their best.

Party Room

Our party rooms provide the perfect setting for any gathering, whether it’s an intimate gathering or a grand event. With elegant décor, customizable layouts, and full service, we ensure your occasion is as memorable as your vision.

My Event

My Event is a platform dedicated to event management, whether for corporate or personal occasions. We support you throughout the entire process, from planning to execution, with easy-to-use tools designed to make every event a success
